About Us

A few words about us

We bring you the most exotic trips at most affordable prices.

If you are a traveler who skips luxurious tours to enjoy local culture of the place, enjoy staying and befriending with local people then you are at right place. For making our trips economical, we try to use public mode of transport, stay in village homes or just camp. All these after extensive research on the place. For enjoying our trips, you need to be open-minded and keep your luxurious expectations low. Participant who are fit to walk 7-8 km are expected to join. There are no age restrictions.

We are truly backpackers because

• We prefer to stay in village homes than hotels • We prefer to try local cuisines in remote places • We try to learn more from local villagers and respect their culture • We never leave our plastics and papers behind • We appreciate new suggestions from our participants • Yes, we are much more cost effective than the usual travel organizers
